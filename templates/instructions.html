📱 Insta Follower Analyzer

📝 How to Use:

  1. Open your Instagram profile on a computer browser.
  2. Click on More → Your Activity → Download Your Information.
  3. Select **HTML format**, and submit the request.
  4. You will receive an email link to download your data.
  5. Download and unzip the file.
  6. Open the folder followers_and_following/.
  7. Get the files:
    • followers.html
    • following.html
  8. Next, upload both files in the tool!
